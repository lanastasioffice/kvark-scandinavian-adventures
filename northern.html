Abisko National Park, Kiruna, Sweden

We fly into Kiruna Airport and drive 1 1/2 hours to Abisko National Park. There we board our 100 meter sailing ship, The Nordic Queen, for 3 days and 2 nights of cuising along the fjord. Keep your ears open for the chimes - they tell you when to look up onto the skies for spectacular sights. During the day, we take excursions into the park to hike, showshoe and crosscounty ski.
